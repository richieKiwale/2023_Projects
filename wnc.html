About Us


Since 2019

Winning Nature in Charisma is a non-governmental organization that was founded by Kelvin Emmanuel Mkude in 2019 and registered on 13 April of 2022.

Our motto

“Difference is Value”

This is to encourage the person with different disability to accept themselves that they have slight differences from the rest but those differences are what gives them their value, thus the purpose of the establishment of the organization is to channel the right positive winning attitudes and mindset by sharpening the charisma and lifestyles of persons with disability. After all, what is the use for one to be same as everyone.

Our Insights

Vision

To be an exemplary non-governmental organization that puts an emphases on having a society that does not judge persons with disability basing on their disabilities.


Mission

To prepare conducive environment and different platforms that will enable persons with disabilities to showcase their abilities basing on their talents and professions.
